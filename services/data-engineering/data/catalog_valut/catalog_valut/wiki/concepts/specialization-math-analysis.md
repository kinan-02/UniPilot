# Mathematical Analysis for Data Science Specialization (מגמת אנליזה מתמטית למדעי הנתונים)

**Available within:** [[track-data-information-engineering]] (B.Sc. only)  
**Total credits required:** 26 credits (5 required base courses = 19 credits + electives)  
**Certificate:** Signed by the Dean upon completion  
**Faculty:** [[faculty-dds]]

## Description

The Mathematical Analysis for Data Science specialization expands the mathematical foundations, techniques, and methodologies frequently used in theoretical and applied data science research. The specialization combines selected mathematical courses with theoretical data science courses (game theory, statistics, machine learning, deep learning, probability theory, optimization) as part of the DNE degree.

The combination provides students with advanced analytical and theoretical capabilities that complement and strengthen their engineering training. Students also have the opportunity to participate in research projects supervised by faculty members.

## Admission

- Based on: psychometric score (סכם) + personal interview
- Must apply **before the start of studies**
- Late admission allowed until beginning of **Semester 3**

## Requirements

- **26 credit points** total:
  - **5 required base courses** (19 credits)
  - Additional credits from the electives list
- Students who take courses beyond the minimum may count the excess toward their M.Sc. degree later, provided they satisfy both B.Sc. and specialization requirements.

### תיאור

מגמת אנליזה מתמטית למדעי הנתונים מרחיבה את היסודות, הטכניקות והמתודולוגיות המתמטיות הנפוצות במחקר מדעי הנתונים התיאורטי והיישומי. המגמה משלבת קורסים מתמטיים נבחרים עם קורסי מדעי נתונים תיאורטיים (תורת המשחקים, סטטיסטיקה, למידת מכונה, למידה עמוקה, הסתברות, אופטימיזציה).
