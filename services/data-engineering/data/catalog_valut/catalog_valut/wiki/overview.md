## 1. University-Wide Structures

### Undergraduate Regulations
All Technion BSc programs governed by [[regulations-undergraduate]]:
- Minimum pass grade: **55** (1–100 scale)
- Degree-average requirement: **65**
- 8 conditions for non-regular (על תנאי) academic standing
- Honors threshold: average ≥85 with no failures
- English proficiency requirement for all students
- Physical education (חינוך גופני): 2 credits mandatory

### Graduate Regulations
Governed by [[regulations-graduate]]:
- MSc (מוסמך): thesis or non-thesis (project) tracks
- Direct Doctorate available in many faculties (no prior MSc required)
- Typical admission GPA: 80–90 depending on program
- Time limits: MSc ≤3 years, PhD ≤5 years (extensions possible)
- Tuition scholarships and research fellowships widely available

### Student Rights & Accommodations
- [[student-rights]] — Student Rights Law 5767-2007 (anti-discrimination, ombudsman §22)
- [[student-discipline-regulations]] — Disciplinary Code (2016/2018): 20+ offenses, 3-tier judiciary
- [[reserve-service-adaptations]] — Reserve duty: special exam within 45 days, extensions
- [[pregnancy-fertility-adaptations]] — 6-week post-birth leave, 25% exam time extension

### Academic Calendar
[[academic-calendar]] — Full 2025/2026 calendar: Semester A (Oct–Feb), Semester B (Mar–Jul), Summer, exam periods, Jewish holidays.

### Cross-Faculty Specializations
[[technion-specializations]] — Entrepreneurial Leadership Minor (יזמות מנהיגות, 10 credits, cross-faculty).
