# 02740635 — Research Thesis (M.D.)

**Hebrew name:** עבודת גמר מחקרית
**Credits:** none (required for M.D. — no credit assigned)
**Required in:** [[track-medicine-md]]
**Prerequisites:** none (proposal must be submitted by start of year 5 of the clinical program)
**Required in:** [[track-medicine-md]]

## Description

A mandatory research thesis required for receiving the M.D. degree. Students must submit a thesis proposal by the start of year 5 (the fifth year of studies, i.e., the second clinical year). Completion of all academic obligations including the final thesis grade is required before authorization to begin the internship (סטאז').
