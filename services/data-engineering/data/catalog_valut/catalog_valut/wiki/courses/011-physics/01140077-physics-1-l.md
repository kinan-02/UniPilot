# 01140077 — Physics 1 L

**Hebrew name:** פיזיקה 1 ל'
**Credits:** 2.5 נ"ז
**Prerequisites:** none
**Required in:** [[track-biology-general]], [[track-biology-human-development]], [[track-biotechnology-food-engineering]], [[track-chemical-engineering]], [[track-chemistry-chemistry]]

## Description

Course for students without physics-mechanics classification. Contains an additional non-credited component of three lecture hours and two tutorial hours per week. Students with physics-mechanics classification may substitute Physics 1 (114051) or Physics 1M (114071).
