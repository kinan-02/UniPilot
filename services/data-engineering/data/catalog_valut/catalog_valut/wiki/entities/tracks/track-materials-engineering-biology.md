## Overview

Joint program offered by [[faculty-materials-science-engineering]] and [[faculty-biology]]. Combines materials science and biological science, targeting students interested in biomaterials, tissue engineering, and bio-inspired materials. Includes biology (general, biochemistry, microbiology) integrated with materials engineering.
