# Mechanical Engineering — Barak (Atuda) Track

**Faculty:** [[faculty-mechanical-engineering]]
**Total Credits:** ~155 נ"ז

## Overview

Accelerated military-academic track (Atuda) for IDF cadets studying mechanical engineering at [[faculty-mechanical-engineering]]. Follows the standard mechanical engineering curriculum with adaptations for military service obligations. Students serve in the IDF and complete their B.Sc. in a combined academic-military framework.
