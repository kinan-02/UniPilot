# Mapping and Geo-Information Engineering (4-Year)

**Faculty:** [[faculty-civil-environmental-engineering]]
**Program code:** 001024-1-000
**Total Credits:** 157 נ"ז

## Overview

Four-year engineering track in [[faculty-civil-environmental-engineering]] covering geodesy, photogrammetry, remote sensing, GIS, and satellite navigation. Graduates receive the degree M.Sc. in Mapping and Geo-Information Engineering and are eligible for registration in the Israeli Engineers Register. Includes both mapping specialization chain and cadastre + land management chain.
