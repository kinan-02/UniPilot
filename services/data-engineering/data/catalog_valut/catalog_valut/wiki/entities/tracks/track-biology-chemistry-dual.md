# Biology and Chemistry Dual Track

**Faculty:** [[faculty-biology]] + [[faculty-chemistry]]
**Program code:** 012124-1-000
**Degree:** B.Sc. in Biology and Chemistry (dual-department)
**Total Credits:** ~135 נ"ז

## Overview

Three-year dual-department program offering deep training in both biology and chemistry. Graduates receive the degree "Bachelor of Science in Biology and Chemistry — dual-department format." Students can continue to advanced degrees in either biology or chemistry.

The program combines a solid foundation in both disciplines, enabling graduates to work at the intersection of chemistry and biology — including biochemistry, pharmaceutical chemistry, and molecular biology research.
