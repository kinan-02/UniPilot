## Faculty Elective Requirements

Students must choose ≥24 credits across ≥3 of the seven elective clusters, with ≥8 credits per chosen cluster. Additional credits may be drawn from any faculty electives or from a list of other-faculty courses (≤9 credits from other faculties). A course used as a Libbah core course cannot also count as a cluster elective.

## Special Rules

- Students choosing the Propulsion and Energy Conversion cluster (Cluster 5) must complete both 00850406 (Rocket Propulsion) and 00850407 (Jet Engines).
- Students choosing the Design and Systems Engineering cluster (Cluster 7) must complete 00840143 (Aerospace Systems Engineering).
- Course 00440102 (Electrical Lab Safety) is mandatory before performing labs in 00440098 (Intro to Electrical Engineering for Aerospace). It consists of 4 lecture hours, one-time, no credit awarded.
- Elective cluster list is updated periodically; check faculty website and secretary's office for updates.
- Faculty electives from other faculties may not exceed 9 credits total.
- A course used as a Libbah (core) course cannot simultaneously count toward an elective cluster.
- The seminar choice (Semester 7) and experimental methods lab (Semester 6) are recommended to align with chosen elective clusters.
- Intro to Aerospace Engineering (00850201) may only be taken in the first semester of studies.
