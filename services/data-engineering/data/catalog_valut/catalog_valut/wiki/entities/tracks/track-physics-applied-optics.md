# Physics — Applied Optics Specialization

**Faculty:** [[faculty-physics]]
**Program code:** 011155-1-000
**Degree:** M.Sc. in Physics (Applied Optics specialization)
**Total Credits:** 155 נ"ז
**Code:** 011 (Physics) + Applied Optics מגמה

## Overview

Four-year applied optics specialization track within the Physics BSc/MSc program. Provides the theoretical-experimental foundations of modern optics and its applications in advanced technology industries and research. Students may apply in semester 3 of the standard three-year physics track.

The specialization integrates optical physics, laser physics, electro-optics, and nano-optics. Graduates are equipped to work in photonics, optical communications, and laser technology.
