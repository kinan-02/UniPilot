## Elective Rotations (אלקטיבים) — 8 weeks required

Students must complete 8 weeks of elective rotations during the 3 clinical years. Each elective is 1 week in duration. Rotations may only be done at departments in hospitals affiliated with the Technion. A published list of approved departments is released each year. Up to 2 weeks may be a free-choice elective (בחירה חופשית קלינית), subject to prior faculty approval.

Elective subjects available include (partial list): immunology, rheumatology, infectious diseases, pulmonology, endocrinology, pain medicine, surgical pathology, palliative care, pediatric orthopedics, pediatric endocrinology, public health, medical genetics, pediatric gastroenterology, pediatric oncology, pediatric ICU, trauma, neonatology, oral surgery, medical administration, pediatric neurology, pediatric nephrology, pediatric psychiatry, clinical pharmacology, pediatric cardiology, pediatric rheumatology, pediatric pulmonology, nuclear medicine, emergency medicine, occupational medicine, general rehabilitation, laboratory medicine, pediatric surgery, vascular surgery, neurosurgery, gastroenterology, clinical hematology, nephrology, dermatology and venereology, emergency medicine, urological surgery, plastic surgery, thoracic/cardiac surgery, internal medicine, surgery, psychiatry, gynecology, neurology, diagnostic radiology, geriatrics, clinical cardiology, oncology, orthopedic surgery, anesthesia and intensive care, ophthalmology, ENT and head/neck surgery, integrative medicine, dental medicine, and more.

## Research Thesis (עבודת גמר מחקרית)

A research thesis is a mandatory requirement for the M.D. degree. Students must submit a thesis proposal by the start of year 5. Completion of all academic obligations (including the final thesis grade) is required before authorization to begin the internship (סטאז'). The thesis procedure is published on the faculty website.

## Academic Regulations (Clinical Years)

### General
- The clinical curriculum is mandatory. Only courses in the clinical program may be registered.
- Clinical years are **annual** (not semester-based). Leave of absence = one full academic year.
- To advance to the next clinical year, all academic obligations of the current year must be completed.
- Teaching hours follow departmental schedule. Students perform one duty shift per week per department requirements.
- Free elective (בחירה חופשית) of up to 2 weeks total across all clinical years, subject to prior faculty approval; must be medical/research or community-service oriented.

### Attendance
- Full attendance is required in all clinical year activities, including frontal lectures (e.g., introductory courses).
- Year 4 (שנה ד'): up to 20% absence permitted in Systems Integration lectures only; no absence from PBL/isotopes/geriatrics tour or equivalent.
- Rotations (סבבים): up to 10% absence permitted (in coordination with the department). Absence > 10% requires repeating the rotation.

### Examinations
- All students must take exams on their first scheduled date (מועד א'). Only students who attended and failed מועד א' are eligible for מועד ב' (with limited exceptions).
- Not appearing for any exam date = failure.
- Pass mark for rotation final exams and all components: **70** (unless otherwise specified).
- Pass mark for Systems Integration (שילוב מערכות): **65** in each of the 3 system clusters. Failure in any cluster = failure in the entire course, requiring repetition of year 4.
- Pass mark for final licensing exams: **60**. Two failures in the same subject → mandatory full group rotation repeat; four failures → faculty recommends dismissal from M.D. program.
- Exams may take place any weekday (no Saturday).
- Oral exams: arrive at least 15 minutes early; no time extensions.
- Written/online exams: no entry permitted ≥ 30 minutes after start.

### Advisory Committee (ועדת יעוץ ומעקב)
The committee meets to review student academic status during or at the end of the clinical division. The committee will recommend dismissal from the M.D. program in the following cases:
- Repeated failure in year 4 (failure in the repeat year).
- Second failure in the same required subject.
- Three failures in required subjects across the clinical years (including behavioral/professional failures).
- Four exam failures in the same national licensing exam subject.

### Graduation
- To qualify for the M.D., students must complete at least 6 years of study and fulfill all academic obligations (all rotations, practice exams, final exams, thesis grade, etc.).
- The M.D. graduation ceremony is held once a year in June. Students must submit the final degree closure request by March 10 of the relevant year.
- After graduation, the student must complete a **one-year internship (שנת סטאז')** as required by the Ministry of Health to receive a permanent medical license in Israel.

#### תנאי קבלה לחטיבה הקלינית

מעבר לחטיבה הקלינית דורש עמידה בכל תנאי המעבר כמפורט בתקנון השנים הפרה-קליניות וסגירת תואר BSc במדעי הרפואה.

#### מהלך הלימודים

במהלך השנים הקליניות נלמדים מקצועות חובה, מקצועות בחירה (אלקטיבים) ומתקיימים מבחני גמר ארציים. השנים הקליניות נלמדות באופן שנתי ולא סמסטריאלי.

**ניתן ללמוד את מקצועות החובה הקצרים "על פנימית" ו"על כירורגיה" רק לאחר מעבר בהצלחה של סבב פנימית וכירורגיה בהתאמה.**

#### עבודת גמר מחקרית (עג"מ)

במהלך הלימודים יבצע סטודנט עבודת גמר המהווה חלק מהדרישות לקבלת התואר MD. על הסטודנט להגיש הצעה לעבודת גמר עד תחילת שנה חמישית.

#### תנאי קבלת תואר MD

לטובת קבלת תואר M.D., על הסטודנט להשלים 6 שנות לימוד לפחות ולעמוד בכל החובות האקדמיים (לרבות השלמת כלל הסבבים, בחינות המתכונת, בחינות הגמר, ציון על עבודת הגמר וכיו"ב). מילוי חובות אלה מזכה את הסטודנט בתעודת 'דוקטור לרפואה' מטעם הטכניון.

#### סטאז'

בתום השנים הקליניות על הבוגר להשלים שנת סטאז' בהתאם לנהלי משרד הבריאות, על מנת לקבל רישיון קבוע לעסוק ברפואה בישראל.
