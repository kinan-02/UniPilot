# Physics + Electrical Engineering — Joint B.Sc./M.Sc.

**Faculty:** [[faculty-physics]]
**Total Credits:** 159-178 נ"ז

## Overview

Joint program shared between [[faculty-physics]] and [[faculty-electrical-computer-engineering]], designed for exceptional students. Students take the combined curriculum for the first three years, then choose to complete either: (a) B.Sc. in Physics (159 credits), (b) M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering (162 credits), or (c) Combined M.Sc. in both Physics and EE (178 credits). Students may also continue to a direct Ph.D.

**פקולטה:** [[faculty-physics]]
