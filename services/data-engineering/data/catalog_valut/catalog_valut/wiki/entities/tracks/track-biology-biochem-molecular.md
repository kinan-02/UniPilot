# Biology — Biochemistry and Molecular Biology Specialization

**Faculty:** [[faculty-biology]]
**Total Credits:** ~126.5 נ"ז

## Overview

Specialization within the biology B.Sc. focusing on biochemistry and molecular biology. Trains students for research at the forefront of molecular life sciences. Core includes general biology, genetics, cell biology, biochemistry, metabolic pathways, and advanced molecular biology lab courses. Students may apply to the excellence (honors) track for direct PhD admission.
