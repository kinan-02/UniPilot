## Overview

Three-year accelerated track in [[faculty-civil-environmental-engineering]] awarding a B.Sc. in Mapping and Geo-Information (not a full engineering degree). Designed for students who want a shorter study path. Does not include all engineering qualifying courses. Graduates with this degree may continue to the full four-year M.Sc. program.
