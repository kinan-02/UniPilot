# Dual Degree Biomedical Engineering and Medicine BSc Track

**Faculty:** [[faculty-biomedical-engineering]] + [[faculty-medicine]]
**Program Code:** 027399-1-000
**Degrees Granted:**
  - בוגר למדעים (BSc) במדעי הרפואה (Medical Sciences)
  - בוגר למדעים (BSc) בהנדסה ביו-רפואית (Biomedical Engineering)
  - Optional continuation: MD (Doctor of Medicine) upon completion of 3 additional clinical years
**Duration:** 5 years (pre-clinical, 10 semesters) + 3 clinical years for MD
**Total Credits (pre-clinical):** 238.0

## Eligibility and Motivation

This track is designed for **outstanding students with especially high psychometric scores** who are admitted to medical studies and wish to simultaneously complete a degree in biomedical engineering.

The goal is to train graduates with deep expertise in both engineering and medicine, capable of integration and leadership in either field independently, as well as in research, development, and industry requiring knowledge of both.

Modern medicine, in both its diagnostic and therapeutic aspects, is becoming increasingly complex. High-tech industry continues to develop at a rapid pace — today approximately half of Israel's startup companies work on developing medical and biological methods and products.

## Admissions and Structure

- Students admitted to medicine who wish to study biomedical engineering in addition
- First years: basis in exact sciences, engineering, life sciences, and medicine
- Later in the program: medical subjects studied in parallel with biomedical engineering subjects
- Students who meet the conditions for transition to the clinical track continue for 3 more years and receive an **MD degree** (per medicine faculty regulations)
- After receiving MD, a one-year internship (סטאז') must be completed per Ministry of Health requirements for a medical license in Israel

**Important rule:** Medical courses that are parallel to BME courses will only be credited toward the dual degree for students who complete the **full dual-degree program**. Completing only one degree requires full fulfillment of that degree's requirements independently.

## Elective Requirements

### Engineering Faculty Electives (21.5 credits)

- Minimum **21.5 credits** from BME faculty elective courses
- Must complete at least **one elective group** from BME domain areas per group rules
- At least **5 of the elective courses** from the Faculty of BME (33x0xxx)
- **Special rule (this track only):** Courses 3350002 BME Lab 2 and 3360022 From Cell to Tissue count as engineering faculty electives

### Medicine Electives (4.0 credits)
- Minimum **4.0 credits** from medicine elective courses (e.g., Systemic Pathology 2 — 2740328+related)

⁵ Faculty elective rules: draw from BME elective groups (see [[track-biomedical-engineering]] for full group listings). At least one complete BME elective group required.

## MD Continuation Track

Students who meet the transition conditions to the clinical track (3 additional years):
- Continue for 3 years of clinical training
- Upon completion, receive MD degree (per medicine faculty regulations — [[faculty-medicine]])
- Internship year (סטאז') required per Ministry of Health before receiving license to practice medicine in Israel

## Special Rules

1. Medical courses parallel to BME courses credited toward dual degree **only** for students completing the full dual program.
2. Completing only one degree requires independently fulfilling all that degree's requirements.
3. Some courses offered once a year forming chains — follow recommended plan to avoid delays.
4. Study plan may change — update at start of each academic year.
5. Technical English Advanced B is counted in mandatory credits; must be completed by end of Semester 4.
