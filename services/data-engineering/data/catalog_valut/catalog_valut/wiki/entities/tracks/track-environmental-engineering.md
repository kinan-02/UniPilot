## Overview

Standalone B.Sc. track in [[faculty-civil-environmental-engineering]] training engineers in environmental systems. Covers water resources, air quality, soil contamination, aquatic ecosystems, environmental microbiology, and environmental management. Graduates work in environmental consulting, regulation, and research. Degree: M.Sc. in Environmental Engineering.
