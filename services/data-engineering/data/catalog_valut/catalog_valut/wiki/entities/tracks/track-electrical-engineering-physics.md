# Electrical Engineering and Physics Track — Electrical and Computer Engineering

**Faculty:** [[faculty-electrical-computer-engineering]] (joint with Faculty of Physics)
**Degree:** מוסמך למדעים בהנדסת חשמל (B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering) — EE-Physics specialization noted on transcript
**Program Code:** 004141-1-000
**Total Credits Required:**
- **BSc (EE-Physics major):** 162 נ"ז
- **BSc (Physics-EE major):** 159 נ"ז (via Faculty of Physics)
- **Combined BSc + MSc (dual degree):** 178 נ"ז

## Program Overview

This is a **joint program** between the Faculty of Electrical and Computer Engineering and the Faculty of Physics. It prepares engineers with exceptionally deep knowledge in physics, complementing and expanding the scientific-technological knowledge acquired in the ECE faculty. The program is characterized by an **accelerated pace** and is designed for **outstanding students**. Admission is through a **separate admissions track**.

## Two Sub-Tracks

### Sub-Track A: Accelerated BSc+MSc (תוכנית לימודים מואצת לתואר ראשון ושני — 004141-1-000)
Enables completion of both BSc and research MSc within **5 years**, with a full scholarship in years 4 and 5.

- In the first three years, students follow the combined BSc program.
- After year 3, students can begin research MSc studies with full scholarship (subject to meeting admissions requirements and faculty recommendation).
- BSc completion: students choose between "מוסמך למדעים בהנדסת חשמל במגמה להנדסת חשמל ופיזיקה" (162 נ"ז) or "מוסמך למדעים בפיזיקה במגמה לפיזיקה והנדסת חשמל" (159 נ"ז).
- Alternative: Complete the **combined dual BSc+MSc** degree (178 נ"ז).
- By beginning of year 4: must find a research supervisor. By end of year 4: must submit thesis research proposal.
- MSc in ECE (from EE-Physics BSc): 16 additional credits + 2 credits English expanded.
- MSc in ECE (from Physics-EE BSc): 19 additional credits + 2 credits English expanded.
- MSc in Physics: 16 additional credits + 2 credits English expanded.

### Sub-Track B: Combined BSc+MSc Dual Degree (מסלול משולב לתואר מוסמך למדעים בהנדסת חשמל ובפיזיקה)
Graduates receive "מוסמך למדעים בהנדסת חשמל ובפיזיקה" — a combined degree.
Total: **178 נ"ז**
- Required courses: 136 נ"ז
- Faculty electives: 30.0 נ"ז (at least 22 in EE, at least 5 in Physics)
- General Technion electives: 12 נ"ז

## Elective Requirements

1. Within EE electives: must complete **2 different specialization groups** (a double specialization group may be combined with a regular one).
2. Physics elective courses that appear in a specialization group may be counted toward either EE or Physics credits — student's choice.
3. Physics electives may be chosen from: Physics elective list, or Physics required courses that are not required in this track (List 3 of the three-year physics program).
4. At least one physics elective must be chosen from:
   - 01160210 — אופטיקה (3.5 נ"ז)
   - 01160029 — מבוא לביופיזיקה (3.5 נ"ז)
   - 01160031 — מבוא לאינפורמציה וחישוב קוונטים (3.5 נ"ז)
   - 01160354 — אסטרופיזיקה וקוסמולוגיה (3.5 נ"ז)
   - 01160004 — פיזיקה של גרעינים וחלקיקים יסודיים (3.5 נ"ז)
   - 01160027 — תורת הרצף (3.5 נ"ז)
5. For the BSc+MSc combined degree (178 נ"ז): students who took 01140246 (אלקטרומגנטיות ואלקטרודינמיקה) — required for physics MSc — get 1.5 credits as physics elective (minimum physics elective drops to 3.5 נ"ז). Students who took 00440140 (שדות אלקטרומגנטיים) instead must take 1.5 additional physics elective credits (minimum 6.5 נ"ז from physics).
