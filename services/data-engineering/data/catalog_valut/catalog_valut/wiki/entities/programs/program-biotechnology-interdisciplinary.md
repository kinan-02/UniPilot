# Biotechnology (Interdisciplinary Program) — Technion

**Program code:** 82
**Degrees granted:** מגיסטר למדעים בביוטכנולוגיה (MSc), דוקטור לפילוסופיה (PhD)
**Level:** Graduate (Master's and Doctorate)

> **Note:** This is the *inter-faculty* interdisciplinary program in Biotechnology. It is distinct from the Faculty of Biotechnology and Food Engineering (הנדסת ביוטכנולוגיה ומזון), which offers undergraduate and graduate degrees in its own right.

## Program Overview

Technion, across its various units, conducts extensive activities in biotechnology. To institutionalize this activity, an interdisciplinary program was established. Within this framework, students can study toward either a "Master of Science in Biotechnology" or a "Doctor of Philosophy" degree. The studies and research conducted within this program combine various aspects of life sciences and engineering, together with specialization in a specific area.

The program is open — as befits an interdisciplinary field — to students from engineering faculties and from science faculties alike.

## Admission Requirements

### Master's (MSc)

- Cumulative GPA of **85 or above** in qualifying studies
- Must meet the Graduate School's admission requirements
- Graduates of engineering faculties or science faculties are eligible
- A prerequisite for admission is **finding a supervisor** from among the faculty members in the various participating units
- Approval of the research topic — based on an abstract — by the program committee
- Research topics must incorporate biotechnological/engineering aspects

### Doctorate (PhD)

- A second degree (Master's) with appropriate background and GPA of **85 or above** in both courses and thesis
- Outstanding MSc students with excellent achievements and research distinction may transfer to the direct doctoral track toward PhD
- Admission is conditional on finding a supervisor, completing an admission interview, and defining a research area

## Study Track

The master's track is a **research track** (עבודת מחקר) — thesis-based.

## Program Structure and Credit Requirements

### Master's (MSc)

**4-year bachelor's graduates:**
- 20 advanced course credits
- 2 credits — Extended English (אנגלית מורחבת)
- 20 credits — Research thesis
- **Total: 42 credits**

**3-year bachelor's graduates:**
- 20 supplementary credits (השלמות)
- 20 advanced course credits
- 2 credits — Extended English
- 20 credits — Research thesis
- **Total: 62 credits**

The supplementary credits will focus particularly on engineering and technology areas. Advanced courses will come from both engineering and biotechnology fields. Course registration requires supervisor recommendation and program committee approval.

Where a specific course is unavailable, an equivalent course — in life sciences or engineering respectively — may be substituted, with supervisor recommendation and committee approval.

Research work will be conducted in one of the academic units participating in the program. The research topic must incorporate both life-science and engineering aspects, and must be approved by the biotechnology committee.

**Note:** Course 3280017 "Academic Writing for Master's Students" (כתיבה אקדמית למגיסטרנטים) is not counted in the credit accumulation. Students choosing to take it receive two additional credits added to the study requirement.

### Doctorate (PhD)

- Coursework of **8–11 credits** (general scope) for broadening horizons and supplementing the basic knowledge required for research
- Candidates lacking a life sciences background will additionally be required to complete **4–8 extra prerequisite/supplementary credits**
- Candidates lacking an engineering background may be required to complete up to **8 additional credits** at the supervisor's discretion
- Passing a **candidacy examination** — including a general part and a part related to a research proposal that advances knowledge in the field and demonstrates findings worthy of scientific publication
- Carrying out research work as required by Graduate School regulations
- Writing and submitting a thesis and passing a final oral examination

## Special Notes

- The program is explicitly cross-disciplinary: admission is open to both engineering and science faculty graduates
- The research topic must bridge life-science and engineering aspects — pure single-domain topics are not eligible
- Research must be conducted at one of the participating academic units

## נתונים בעברית

**התכנית הבין-יחידתית לביוטכנולוגיה — תכנית 82**

תכנית בין-יחידתית בהשתתפות היחידות הבאות: הנדסה אזרחית, הנדסה כימית, הנדסת ביוטכנולוגיה ומזון, כימיה, ביולוגיה, רפואה, הנדסה ביו-רפואית, פיזיקה.

**תנאי הקבלה למגיסטר:** ציון ממוצע מצטבר של 85 לפחות בלימודי הסמכה. תנאי לקבלה הוא מציאת מנחה מבין חברי הסגל ואישור נושא המחקר. נושאי המחקר חייבים לשלב היבטים ביוטכנולוגיים/הנדסיים.

**דרישות הלימוד למגיסטר:**
- לבוגרי תואר ראשון ארבע-שנתי: 20 נקודות מתקדמים + 2 נק' אנגלית מורחבת + 20 נק' עבודת מחקר (סה"כ 42 נקודות).
- לבוגרי תואר ראשון תלת-שנתי: 20 נקודות השלמה + 20 נקודות מתקדמים + 2 נק' אנגלית מורחבת + 20 נק' עבודת מחקר (סה"כ 62 נקודות).

**תנאי הקבלה לדוקטורט:** תואר שני עם ממוצע ציונים של 85 ומעלה הן בקורסים והן בתיזה. הקבלה מותנית במציאת מנחה, בראיון קבלה ובהגדרת תחום מחקר.

**דרישות הלימוד לדוקטורט:** 8–11 נקודות מתקדמות, עמידה בבחינת מועמדות, ביצוע עבודת מחקר, כתיבה והגשת תזה.
