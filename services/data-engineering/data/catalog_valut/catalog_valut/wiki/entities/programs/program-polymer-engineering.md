**Degrees granted:** ME (מגיסטר להנדסה, without thesis), MSc (מגיסטר למדעים, with thesis), PhD (דוקטור לפילוסופיה)

## Program Overview

In light of the development of the plastics industry in Israel, the program was established to meet the demand in various market sectors for engineers and scientists in the field of polymers and plastics. Teaching and research activities in polymer engineering and science can be found in a variety of academic units at Technion.

The interdisciplinary program in Polymer Engineering offers three degrees: ME (without thesis), MSc (with thesis), and PhD. Continued study within the framework of Polymer Engineering enables training for work in the field, for development and research on topics related to polymer production, polymer engineering, and polymer applications.

Students may work while studying (ניתן ללמוד ולעבוד במקביל).

## Admission Requirements

- Graduates of **4-year engineering faculties** (BSc) and graduates of **science faculties** (BA or BSc) may apply
- Graduates of **3-year science programs** require approximately **30 supplementary credits** from programs in Chemical Engineering, Materials Science, or Chemistry, as determined by the interdisciplinary committee; the course list is determined based on the student's background
- Graduates of 4-year programs without relevant background require supplementary credits per the committee's decision
- **ME-to-PhD transition:** ME graduates wishing to pursue a PhD must complete research supplements as "non-degree" students, per the interdisciplinary committee for Polymer Engineering and in accordance with Graduate School regulations

## Program Structure and Credit Requirements

### ME — מגיסטר להנדסה (without thesis)

Total credits: **42**
- 34 course credits (not including optional academic writing course)
  - At least **20 credits** from the Core and Elective course lists (Polymer Engineering and Polymer Science courses)
  - Of those, at least **10 credits** from the Core course list
  - Up to **16 credits** from courses in Management, Economics, Quality Assurance, and Reliability
- 6 credits — Final project / seminar paper
- 2 credits — Extended English (אנגלית מורחבת)

### MSc — מגיסטר למדעים (with thesis)

Total credits: **38**

| Undergraduate degree | Advanced course credits | Supplementary credits |
|---|---|---|
| 4-year engineering or equivalent (relevant background) | 18 | Per committee decision |
| 4-year (other) | 18 | Per committee decision |
| 3-year | 18 | ~30 |

*Advanced course credits include Extended English (2 credits); academic writing elective not included.*

The main requirements:
- At least **12 credits** from Core and Elective course lists, of which at least **6** from the Core list
- 2 credits — Extended English
- Satisfactory research work
- A seminar lecture
- Writing and submission of a master's thesis (**20 credits**) per Graduate School regulations
- Passing a final oral examination

Students in the ME track wishing to transfer to the research MSc track must conduct research per the committee's decision and Graduate School regulations.

### PhD — דוקטור לפילוסופיה

Intended for outstanding students with an affinity for research in polymer science and engineering.

**Admission:**
- Must hold a second degree (with thesis)
- Academic and research achievements must be very good
- Must find a supervisor before submitting the application
- The supervisor is approved by the interdisciplinary committee recommendation and Graduate School dean approval
- Decisions based on transcripts, CV, professional achievements, letters of recommendation, and committee interview

**Outstanding students special track:** Students with a GPA of 90+ in undergraduate studies are offered a special direct-to-doctorate track with a shorter study period.

**Direct transfer:** Outstanding MSc students in studies and research may transfer directly to the doctorate, shortening the overall study period.

**Study requirements:**
- At least **10 advanced course credits** (including mandatory academic writing course), per the recommendations of the admissions and interviewing committees
- Passing a **candidacy examination** based on an original research proposal (written independently within 11 months of admission; direct-track students: within 5 months of track change notification)
- Conducting satisfactory research work
- Delivering a seminar lecture
- Writing and submitting a doctoral dissertation per Graduate School regulations
- Passing a final oral examination
- Meeting Graduate School language requirements

## נתונים בעברית

**התכנית הבין-יחידתית להנדסת פולימרים — תכנית 85**

תכנית בין-יחידתית בהשתתפות היחידות הבאות: הנדסת מכונות, הנדסה כימית, הנדסת ביוטכנולוגיה ומזון, כימיה, מדע והנדסה של חומרים, הנדסה ביו-רפואית.

**תנאי הקבלה:** בוגרי פקולטות הנדסיות (4 שנתיות) בעלי תואר BSc ובוגרי פקולטות מדעיות בעלי תואר BA זכאים להגיש בקשה. ניתן ללמוד ולעבוד במקביל.

**מגיסטר להנדסה (ME):** צבירת 42 נקודות לתואר; 40 נק' במקצועות + 2 נקודות עבור "אנגלית מורחבת". לימוד מקצועות בהיקף 34 נקודות (לא כולל קורס הבחירה בכתיבה אקדמית), לפחות 20 נקודות מרשימות מקצועות היסוד והבחירה, שמהן לפחות 10 נקודות מרשימת מקצועות היסוד. בנוסף, עד 16 נקודות מרשימת מקצועות בנושאי ניהול, כלכלה ואבטחת איכות. הסטודנט חייב בעבודת גמר/פרויקט/עבודה סמינריונית בהיקף 6 נקודות.

**מגיסטר למדעים (MSc):** צבירת 38 נק' לתואר; לפחות 12 נקודות מרשימות מקצועות היסוד והבחירה (לפחות 6 מרשימת היסוד), 2 נק' אנגלית מורחבת, עבודת מחקר 20 נקודות.

**דוקטור לפילוסופיה (PhD):** לפחות 10 נקודות מתקדמות, עמידה בבחינת מועמדות, ביצוע עבודת מחקר, הגשת חיבור לתואר דוקטור.
