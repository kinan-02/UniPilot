# Dual Degree: Medicine & Data Engineering

**Program code:** 027396-1-000  
**Duration:** 5 years (pre-clinical) + 3 additional years for clinical M.D.  
**Offered by:** [[faculty-dds]] in collaboration with the Faculty of Medicine  
**Degrees conferred:**
- בוגר למדעים (B.Sc.) בהנדסת נתונים ומידע
- בוגר למדעים (B.Sc.) במדעי הרפואה
- After 3 additional clinical years: M.D. (Doctor of Medicine) — requires intern year per Ministry of Health

## Description

The program prepares graduates with deep knowledge in both Data Engineering and Medicine, who can integrate and lead in research, development, and industry requiring expertise in both fields. The connection between medicine and data science opens new possibilities for disease research, development of innovative treatments, and improvement of clinical efficiency through AI and machine learning.

## Admission Requirements

- Students who were admitted to the Medical School (Faculty of Medicine)
- Who also wish to pursue a degree in Data & Information Engineering
- Outstanding students with **especially high psychometric scores (סכם גבוה במיוחד)**

## Structure Overview

The dual degree is built on the [[program-alonim]] curriculum for Data & Information Engineering (the accelerated version), with the B.Sc. compressed to 3 years (intensive).

## Important Notes

- Parallel medical courses count toward the dual degree only if the student completes the entire dual-degree program.
- Completing only one of the two degrees requires full satisfaction of that degree's requirements independently.
- Students who pass the clinical transition requirements continue to 3 additional years for M.D.
- After M.D., a one-year internship (סטאז') is required per Ministry of Health regulations.
- The English technical course (3240033) must be completed in Semester 1 if required.
