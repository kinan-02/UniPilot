## Overview

Founded in 1954 at the initiative of Israel's first Prime Minister David Ben-Gurion, the Faculty of Aerospace Engineering is the only faculty for aerospace engineering in Israel. It is responsible for educating generations of aerospace engineers who serve at all levels of engineering and development in Israel's aerospace industries. The faculty's alumni are involved in developing, designing, manufacturing, operating, and maintaining airborne and space systems including aircraft, helicopters, UAVs, missiles, jet and rocket propulsion systems, airborne weapons systems, and satellites.

Israel is a world leader in UAV systems (ranked #1 exporter globally), and maintains a small club of nations capable of developing, building, and launching satellites. Systems like "Arrow" (חץ), "Iron Dome" (כיפת ברזל), and "Magic Wand" (שרביט קסמים) are globally recognized achievements of this faculty's alumni.

## Graduate Programs

The faculty offers MSc and PhD programs with research specializations across:
- **Aerodynamics and Fluid Mechanics** — body aerodynamics, fluid mechanics, ballistics, heat and mass transfer, numerical methods, aeroacoustics, rarefied gas dynamics
- **Structures and Solid Mechanics** — finite elements, composite structures, fatigue and failure, smart structures, metamaterials, 3D-printed structures
- **Guidance, Navigation, and Control** — missile guidance, computer-vision navigation, autonomous navigation, flight control, state estimation, fault detection, networked control, human-machine systems
- **Propulsion and Combustion** — jet propulsion, solid-rocket propulsion, scramjet, gas turbine, combustion processes, electrical propulsion in space

### MSc — "Master of Science in Aerospace Engineering" (מגיסטר למדעים בהנדסת אוירונוטיקה וחלל)
For graduates with a BSc in Aerospace Engineering or equivalent completion courses.
- Minimum GPA in BSc: 83 (based on professional courses only)
- Three study pathways: Research (מחקר), Project (פרויקט), Final Paper (עבודת גמר)
  - Research pathway: 32 credits of courses + 20-credit thesis
  - Project pathway: 24 credits of courses + 20-credit project
  - Final paper pathway: 24 credits of courses + 12-credit final paper
- Duration: 4 semesters (funded), 8 semesters (external)

### MSc — "Master of Science" (מגיסטר למדעים)
For candidates whose BSc is not in Aerospace Engineering and who do not need to complete prerequisites.

### ME — "Master of Engineering in Aerospace Engineering" (מגיסטר להנדסה בהנדסת אוירונוטיקה וחלל)
Coursework only, no thesis. For graduates of the AE faculty who wish to deepen specialization knowledge.
- Minimum GPA: 80
- 36 credits of courses + 6-credit project = 42 total credits
- Must choose a primary research area (≥15 credits), plus ≥12 credits in two other areas, up to 8 credits from other faculties

### ME — "Master of Engineering" (מגיסטר להנדסה)
For candidates without an AE BSc who do not need prerequisites.

### PhD (תואר דוקטור)
- Must hold MSc (with thesis) from recognized university
- Requirements: 11 credits of advanced courses (out of 24 total), including 2 credits "Academic Writing for Doctoral Students"
- Must submit research proposal in first year, pass candidacy exam within one month of proposal
- Duration: 42 months (funded), 12 semesters (external)
- GRE optional but recommended for international applicants

### Completion Requirements for Graduates of Other Engineering Faculties
Students from other engineering backgrounds seeking MSc AE or ME AE must complete 20.5 credits of prerequisite courses:
- 00840311 Incompressible Aerodynamics (3.5 credits)
- 00840312 Compressible Flow (2.5 credits)
- 00840515 Introduction to Elasticity Theory (3.5 credits)
- 00840738 Control Theory (3.0 credits)
- 00840220 Flight Mechanics 1 (2.5 credits)
- 00840221 Flight Mechanics 2 (2.5 credits)
- One of: 00850407 Jet Engines (2.5) or 00850406 Rocket Propulsion (2.5)

## נתונים בעברית

### תיאור הפקולטה

הפקולטה להנדסת אוירונוטיקה וחלל בטכניון פתחה את שעריה בשנת 1954, לאחר שראש הממשלה הראשון של המדינה, מר דוד בן-גוריון, הבין שלא תיתכן עליונות אווירית ישראלית בסביבה העוינת בה הוקמה המדינה ללא עליונות מדעית וטכנולוגית. גם כיום, למעלה מ-61 שנה לאחר היוסדה, זאת הפקולטה היחידה להנדסת אוירונוטיקה וחלל במדינה, והיא נושאת באחריות הכבדה של חינוך דורות של מהנדסי אוירונוטיקה וחלל המשולבים בכל דרגי ההנדסה והפיתוח בתעשיות האוויר-חלל בישראל.

חזונה של הפקולטה הוא ביסוסה כמרכז אקדמי מהשורה הראשונה בעולם באוירונוטיקה ובאסטרונוטיקה, מוקד להרחבת גבולות הדעת והפצת רעיונות וידע במדעי והנדסת תעופה וחלל.
