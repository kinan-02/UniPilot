## Summary

Official curriculum guide for the Faculty of Data Science and Decisions ([[faculty-dds]]) at the Technion for academic year 2025/2026. Covers three undergraduate B.Sc. tracks, five excellence/military programs, two B.Sc. minors, four specializations, and seven graduate degree programs. Also lists the full academic staff including the dean, professors, associate professors, and senior lecturers.

## Key Takeaways

- The faculty offers three four-year B.Sc. tracks (all 155 credits): Data & Information Engineering, Industrial Engineering & Management, Information Systems Engineering.
- The faculty has deep interdisciplinary integration of game theory, behavioral economics, cognitive science, and operations research alongside ML/data engineering.
- Military excellence tracks (Avivim, Alonim) and a high-school fast-track (Barak'at) are offered for outstanding students.
- A dual degree in Data Engineering + Medicine is offered in 5 years in collaboration with the Faculty of Medicine.
- Graduate programs span M.Sc. and Ph.D. in six research areas, plus a non-thesis MBA.
- All full details (graduate program requirements, admission criteria) are on the faculty website: https://dds.technion.ac.il/he/
