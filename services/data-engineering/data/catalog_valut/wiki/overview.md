## What is This Faculty?

The Faculty of Data Science and Decisions (DDS) is one of the newer faculties at the Technion. It brings together three previously distinct engineering disciplines under one roof: data engineering, industrial engineering & management, and information systems engineering. The unifying themes are **data**, **decisions**, and **systems thinking** — with a strong emphasis on AI, machine learning, optimization, and human-behavioral dimensions of technical systems.

## Key Concepts and Structures

- **[[focus-chains]]** — Structured 3-course elective clusters in IEM and ISE (≥1 full chain required)
- **Starred electives (★)** — Data-intensive courses in DNE; ≥2 required from the elective list
- **Science requirement** — DNE only: one course from an approved list ([[0940290-science-elective-dne]])
- **Practicum** ([[0940700-dne-practicum]]) — DNE-only mandatory industrial experience, within first 3 semesters
- **Enrichment credits** — 6 non-faculty elective credits required in DNE
- **PE credits** — 2 physical education credits required in DNE
- **University electives** — 12 credits in IEM and ISE
